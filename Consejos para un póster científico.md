- Bullet points. If you can - never have a sentence that runs over a single line. This is the biggest mistake that 90% of people have. Posters are not a place for large paragraphs of text. Emphasise your intro & aims and the conclusions so someone can easily get your message quickly.

Example:

The problem

- Oncogenic variants in ctDNA (the focus of liquid biopsy) are often found at low frequencies.
- Technological noise is also found at low frequencies, which hinders our capability of detecting true variants.
- We need more strategies

One important solution: UMIs

- UMI tagging allows us to identify PCR duplicates with high fidelity
- Discordant changes can be identified as noise.
- UMI tagging requires specific bioinformatic processing for which there are several approximations that have not been benchmarked properly

Las dos anteriores deben recogerse bajo la introducción. Para encajarlo todo, pueden ponerse en horizontal.
