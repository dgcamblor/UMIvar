## Aceite de oliva virgen extra para freír

> EVOO has previously not been recommended for frying because it has a relatively low smoke point (≈205 °C) compared to other oils (peanut oil ≈225 °C, sunflower oil ≈255 °C, soybean oil ≈242 °C, palm oil ≈227 °C), since it is believed that low smoke point means that fats are oxidized. However, recent studies have shown that the smoke point is not an accurate indicator of oil performance and stability. 
> Extra Virgin Olive Oil's healthy properties are preserved even after heat-induced degradation and transformation during cooking.
> [Cooking with extra-virgin olive oil: A mixture of food components to prevent oxidation and degradation - ScienceDirect](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0924224422000693)
