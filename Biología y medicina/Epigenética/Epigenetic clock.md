Epigenetic clocks are methylation estimators of biological age, a better measurement of aging than chronological age. Epigenetic clocks are constructed by performing penalized regression using CpGs on a variable that is a **surrogate of biological age**. Such surrogate can be cronological age (the case of [[Horvath's clock]]) or other variable related to biological age (for example, a set of physiological variables that the [[Levine's clock]] uses)

- [[Hannum's clock]] (single-tissue DNAm age estimator)
- [[Horvath's clock]] (multi-tissue DNAm age estimator)
- [[Levine's clock]] (DNAm PhenoAge)
- DunedinPACE
- GrimAge
