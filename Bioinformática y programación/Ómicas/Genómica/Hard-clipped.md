**Hard-clipped** bases are bases (in the 5' and 3' extremes of a read) that are not part of an alignment, and they have been excluded from the read sequence (contrary to [[Soft-clipped]] bases).

Hard-clipping may appear when the clipped bases align elsewhere in the reference genome (i.e., chimeric reads).
