The Cancer Genome Atlas (TCGA) is a comprehensive and collaborative effort in cancer research that aims to understand the molecular basis of cancer through the analysis of genomic and molecular data.

The TCGA is comprised of several projects; examples are:

- TCGA-COAD (Colorectal Adenocarcinoma)

Data can be accessed at [GDC](https://portal.gdc.cancer.gov/).

## TCGA access via R

A great package for accessing TCGA (GDC) data is [[TCGAbiolinks]].
