## MarkDuplicates

- `REMOVE_DUPLICATES`: Actually remove the duplicate reads in the output file.


> [!danger] 
> Trimming the 5' end of reads based on quality (not on adapter) may interfere with deduplication.


## CleanSam

CleanSam is used to remove unwanted reads from a BAM file (beyond-end-of-reference reads, unmapped reads, etc.).
