**Stories** on Medium fall into three categories:

- Boosted
- General distribution
- Network only

**Publications** are distributions of stories. Some interesting publications are:

- **Programming and data science**
	- [Towards Data Science](https://towardsdatascience.com/). Sharing concepts, ideas, and code, focused on data science and machine learning.
	- [Better Programming](https://betterprogramming.pub/write-for-us-5c4bcba59397#fddd). Advice for programmers.
	- Geek Culture.
- **Health**
	- [Wise & Well](https://medium.com/wise-well/a-call-for-health-and-wellness-writers-for-a-new-medium-publication-2cca2c66ff74). Science-backed health insights.
