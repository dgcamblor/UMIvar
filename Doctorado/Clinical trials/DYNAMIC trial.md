# Description

They used a [[SafeSeqS]] informed panel. Prior detection in tumor tissue (primary tumor) of somatic mutations in 15 genes (SMAD4, TP53, AKT1, APC, BRAF, CTNNB1, ERBB3, FBXW7, HRAS, KRAS, NRAS, PIK3CA, PPP2R1A, RNF43, POLE). Detection of found mutations in ctDNA using Safe-Seq (see [Supplementary Appendix](https://www.nejm.org/doi/suppl/10.1056/NEJMoa2200075/suppl_file/nejmoa2200075_appendix.pdf)).

# Results

They concluded that, following a ctDNA-guided approach to the treatment of stage II colon cancer post-surgery, adjuvant chemotherapy could be reduced without compromising recurrence-free survival (RFS) [@tieCirculatingTumorDNA2022].
