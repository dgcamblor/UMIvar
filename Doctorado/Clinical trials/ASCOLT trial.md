# Description

Studies from Day et al. They track variants using a panel of 14 genes (AKT1, APC, BRAF, CTNNB1, ERBB3, FBXW7, KRAS, NRAS, PIK3CA, POLE, PPP2R1A, RNF43, SMAD4 and TP53), and following the [[SafeSeqS]] technology.

# Results

Preliminary results:

- Serial detection of ctDNA within 1 year of adjuvant chemotherapy is associated with recurrence in patients with resected CRC (http://dx.doi.org/10.1016/j.annonc.2023.09.1777).
- [Site Unreachable](https://www.annalsofoncology.org/article/S0923-7534(22)02369-9/fulltext).
- 2023. In 291 plasma samples, that were analyzed 59 pts recurred, of whom 32 were ctDNA-positive (sensitivity = 54%). The other patients recurred >12m after final sample collection. The reported specificity was 86% [@day586PMinimalResidual2023]
