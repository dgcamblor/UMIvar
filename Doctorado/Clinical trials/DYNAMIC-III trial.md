# Description

A prospective randomized study in which patients are randomized after surgery to standard-of-care or ctDNA-informed adjuvant treatment in CRC stage III patients. It follows the same approach as [[DYNAMIC trial]] and [[DYNAMIC-Rectal trial]], both in CRC stage II and LARC, respectively.

This study expands upon preliminary results by Tie et al., in a multicenter cohort of 96 patients with CRC stage III, where 3-year RFS was significantly lower in ctDNA-positive patients (47% vs. 76%) [@tieCirculatingTumorDNA2019].
