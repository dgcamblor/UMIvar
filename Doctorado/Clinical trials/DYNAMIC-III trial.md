# Description

A prospective randomized study in which patients are randomized after surgery to standard-of-care or ctDNA-informed adjuvant treatment in CRC stage III patients. It follows the same approach as [[DYNAMIC-II trial]] and [[DYNAMIC-Rectal trial]], both in CRC stage II and LARC, respectively.
