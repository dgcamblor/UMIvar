The study [@luoCirculatingTumorDNA2020b] collected 459 CRC tumor samples from the [[TCGA]] and 754 normal samples from a dataset used in a previous methylation study on aging (GSE40279).

Relevant TCGA projects to this case are:

- [TCGA-COAD](https://portal.gdc.cancer.gov/repository?facetTab=cases&filters=%7B%22op%22%3A%22and%22%2C%22content%22%3A%5B%7B%22op%22%3A%22in%22%2C%22content%22%3A%7B%22field%22%3A%22cases.project.project_id%22%2C%22value%22%3A%5B%22TCGA-COAD%22%5D%7D%7D%5D%7D&searchTableTab=files) (Colon Adenocarcinoma)
- TCGA-READ (Rectum Adenocarcinoma)

The idea is to compare those tumor samples to a control dataset as similar as posible (age, sex). 

After [[Differential methylation (DM) analysis]], a series of CRC-speficic methylation markers can be determined.
